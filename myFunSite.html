Managing Your Time

Time management is crucial for a college student. It can be the difference between getting an assignment done or missing out on mulitple assignments. It is a good habit to create an outline or plan that can be used to follow so that tasks can be accomplished and you are motivated to accomplish them.

Setting Aside Time for Yourself

Even when you drowning in your studies, nothing matters more than your mental health. Be sure to set aside time where you can get away from all the work and relax for a bit. It is more benficial to take breaks than it is to just cram everything in nonstop. Take care of yourselves.
